Exmple of template

You can do var subtitutions defining template-vars with the format: {{var-name}}.

Example.

Subsitute template-var 'msg' passing in the URL: ?msg=value: {{msg}}

Your templates also can have muti-language.

Ways to write multi-language

		1. {{
			lang:{lang}[,default][[Text]]
			lang:{other-lang}[[Text]]
			
			}}
		example: {{lang:es[[Hola]] lang:en,default[[Hello]]}}

		2. {{dic:{cat},{code}}}. It is neccesary to connect with a CloudService of translations. Add conf-var:CloudServiceDictionary and CloudServiceKey

Example.

Add to the API url: /{lang}.. /api/templates/Template/en
